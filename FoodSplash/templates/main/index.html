FoodSplash

FoodSplash is a easy-to-use platform tailored to making the process of donation as seamless as possible, while also reducing the amount of food wastage in the community. That’s why your convenience is our priority.

It's Simple, we collect your donations at sites close to you. Then, we handle the transport and approval of your donations to our various non-profit organization partners, and award you points. Finally, we let you know where your donation went and its impact.

Our Policies

Donation

Making a food donation has never been easier and more efficient.

At the drop site, unless otherwise stated in the event description, standard procedure is for your donation to be associated with an ID that the organization will scan or enter to verify your donation once they have confirmed all guidelines have been followed. After leaving your donation at the designated location, be sure to consult with personal at the event in order to confirm your donation, and be sure to take a photo of the approval page as a receipt of your donation.


    If it is unclear or you are unsure of where to start with donations, here are some general guidelines of items that are typically universally accepted:
  • Non-perishables/canned items
  • Occasionally, food is labeled for safety of general donation, which is a good starting point
  • Soup Kitchens and other immediate donors tend to accept cooked meals or leftovers (assuming general hygienic rules)
    • All donations can be easily verified through our platform should the the organization request it, and upon organization approval, points will be granted to your account within 24 hours.

Points

As incentive for those who donate, we have a points system that rewards everyone who takes part in FoodSplash. For every donation that you make, 10 SplashPoints are earned. Top donators on the leaderboard can redeem these points at the end of the month in exchange for prizes!


User Statement

While FoodSplash is the people's platform to connect you with local organizations, it is the user/contributor’s responsibility to assume all travel/transportation, packaging, and other personal logistics duties unless otherwise stated or provided by the organization.


Privacy

In addition to our user’s convenience, their security and privacy is one of our highest priorities. That’s why all information entered into our platform is strictly confidential, and will never be shared with any outside organizations unless consented. As we develop, cybersecurity is our top concern, and we will be sure to inform all users of any updates as we move along to make the process as convenient as we can.


Legal

The Bill Emerson Good Samaritan Food Donation Act protects you, the donor, and the recipient against liability, except for gross negligence and/or intentional misconduct. What this means is that there are laws the protect you from civil and criminal liability if your donation causes harm to its recipient.



Our Partners

Your donations will be distributed to -and verified by- these partners.


Contact Us

Feel free to contact us at any time! Our team will get in touch with you as soon as possible.

If you need support please give us all details that may help resolve your issue! The more detailed you are, the better support we can give, Thanks!

If you are an organization interested in partnering, please reach out! As an organization, you can gain access to more goods from more people than ever possible!
